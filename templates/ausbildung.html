What is Ausbildung?

Ausbildung is Germany's renowned dual vocational training system. You work at a company while attending a vocational school (Berufsschule), earning a salary throughout the training period of 2–3 years.

After completion, you receive an officially recognized German qualification — opening doors to work anywhere in the EU.
